About Me

Born into a comedy family in Chicago. Experienced culture living in China and Asia. Honed theater and art in NYC. Currently learning to surf in Los Angeles (the industry is slow).

My training includes classical theatre, on‑camera technique, improvisation, and martial arts - placed 2nd in Junior Olympics for Tae Kwon Do and trained stunt-fighting with Chris Cassamassa.

In addition to acting, I enjoy collaborating with directors and fellow artists to write and film independent stories.

When I'm not on-set, you can find me haggling in Mandarin for cheap Chinese knick-knacks, bowling turkeys, practicing tai-chi, or stuck in LA traffic.

Thank you for visiting my site—I hope you enjoy my portfolio and reach out if you’d like to collaborate.
