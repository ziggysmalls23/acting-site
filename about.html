About Me

I’m an actor based in Los Angeles with a passion for bringing stories to life. Over the years I’ve honed my craft through intensive study and a wide range of performances—from independent films and network television to regional theatre and commercial voice‑over work.

My training includes classical theatre, on‑camera technique and improvisation, giving me the flexibility to adapt to any role. Whether portraying a comedic character or sinking into a dramatic scene, I strive to deliver authentic, engaging performances that resonate with audiences.

In addition to acting, I enjoy collaborating with directors and fellow artists to create meaningful work. When I’m not on set or stage, you’ll find me exploring new scripts, practicing dialects or volunteering with arts outreach programs.

Thank you for visiting my site—feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you’d like to collaborate.
