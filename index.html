About Me

I came into this world discolored, blue, choking on fecal matter. My mother says I was born full of shit, so once I returned to a normal color, I became an actor.

I am a lifelong performer & improviser. I started acting in films as a child, then theater, then in college, then at Lee Strasberg in NYC, Second City in Chicago, then moved to Shanghai to become fluent in Mandarin Chinese.

Now, I am here for your next project.
